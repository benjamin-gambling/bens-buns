Ben’s Buns was founded in Whistler, BC during the COVID-19 pandemic when founder Benjamin Gambling discovered his love for baking. Recently made unemployed, he and his partner Alicia decided to start baking their own bread. Before they knew it they had more bread in their kitchen than the local supermarket. Ben reached out to the local community offering the bread to people in need and the rest is history! Local residents started purchasing his bread so he could continue to support the people in need and still to this day that philosophy continues. For every 3 loafs of bread sold by Ben’s Buns we donate 1 to the Whistler Community Center to help people who are suffering from financial hardship.
